Mature Adult Dentistry

Expert, gentle dental care for the 55+

Boise dentist Daron Lind understands that the level of care that it takes to restore your teeth and keep them healthy increases as we get older. And if you haven’t been to the dentist in a while, Dr. Lind will help you get back on track with a treatment plan to address your personal needs. Dr. Lind’s dental career includes over 30 years of handling complex cases, in addition to thousands of hours of continuing education to expand his knowledge into fields such as dental implants, full-mouth reconstruction and more.

Gentle and pain-free Care

Dr. Lind focuses his practice on providing gentle, painfree care in a calm and relaxing environment. We have found that this approach makes it possible for people to get care even if they have had past experiences that were less than pleasant at another dental office.

Full Mouth Reconstruction

Getting older doesn’t have to mean losing the function of your teeth and smile. Dr. Lind and restore a smile to its natural beauty through the use of Dental Implants, crowns, bridges or dentures depending on your situation. He believes that keeping your teeth, and having a healthy smile are very important no matter how old or young you may be.

Schedule a Complimentary Consultation with Dr. Lind

Dr. Lind can determine if a bridge is right for you.

Dr. Lind offers a complimentary consultation for new patients. This is perfect for mature adults who are concerned about their oral health and the state of their teeth, gums and jawbone. The appointment includes:

  • Comprehensive Exam
  • Discuss your treatment options
  • Get your questions answered
  • Get a second opinion on dental work

He will help you decide on the best course of treatment.

Come in for a Complimentary Consultation, and learn exactly how Dr. Lind can help you to restore and preserve your smile.

To schedule an appointment with Dr. Lind, please call us at (208) 846-8847 or request an appointment online.

Keep your teeth as you get older

Dr. Lind has helped thousands of patients preserve their teeth for a healthy, beautiful smile.

Dr. Lind can help you improve your oral health no matter what condition your teeth are in today. He believes that anyone can have and enjoy an attractive smile.

Losing your teeth is not necessarily "part of growing old." It is not natural for your teeth and gums to become diseased and unhealthy. Through proper dental care this can be avoided.

If you would like to learn about preserving your smile or how Dr. Lind can restore your mouth to beauty and health, call us to schedule a complimentary consultation.
