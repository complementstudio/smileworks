Dental Crowns

All porcelain crowns to restore your teeth

Dr. Lind is proud to announce he is now able to provide his patients with same day crowns! No more multiple appointments. No temporary crowns falling off on a Saturday night. Just ONE appointment and you will leave with a happy beautiful smile!

Dr. Lind prefers the use of all porcelain crowns to restore his patients teeth because of the durability of the material and the fact they look completely natural. Dr. Lind strongly feels that, "Good dentistry should not be seen or felt in the mouth," and he works hard to practice what he preaches.

Put yourself in Dr. Lind's experienced hands

Having been in practice for over 30 years, Dr. Lind has seen just about every dental situation imaginable. Most importantly, he puts the same amount of care into a single filling as he does into a complete smile makeover.

Dr. Lind has crafted and placed thousands of dental crowns and is specially trained in many specialist facets of dentistry including Dental Implants, Cosmetic Dentistry and Orthodontics. Why might I need a crown?

You may need a crown because of:

  • Extensive tooth decay
  • Large metal or tooth-colored fillings, that weaken the remaining tooth
  • Broken or cracked teeth
  • A tooth that has had root canal therapy
  • A misaligned or misshaped tooth
  • Teeth that have large fillings that are breaking down and cannot be refilled

Sometimes a crown is not the optimum solution and other dental procedures such as porcelain veneers or dental bonding are more appropriate.
