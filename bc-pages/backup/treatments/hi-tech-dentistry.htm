Panoramic Digital x-rays

Panoramic Digital x-rays provide a full view of your mouth rapidly and painlessly, and with much less radiation than traditional xrays.

Our Panorex system produces a single picture of all your teeth and surrounding bones. Sometimes referred to as a full mouth X-ray, this system will let Dr. Lind see anything in your mouth that could affecting you adversely and ensure your treatment is completely tailored to your needs.

Intraoral camera

Intraoral cameras are tiny digital cameras that fit comfortably in your mouth and enable your Dr. Lind to show you an enlarged image of your smile on a computer screen.

Now you can look inside too and see what the dentist is going to fix before he does it.
