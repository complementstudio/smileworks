Emergency Dentist

If you are in pain, call our offices right away at (208) 846-8847. We will make every effort to get you in and seen by the dentist right away.

Life can be unpredictable. In our experience, the only thing most dental emergencies have in common is that they tend to happen when it is inconvenient to get them handled.

A dental emergency can be the result of an accident or injury, but it can also be the result of needing to get treatment that you have been putting off for a while. If you are hurting or have had an accident, it is important that you receive treatment right away.

Whatever the reason, Dr. Lind will do what he can to see you the same day and get you out of pain.
